Impact levels

Use these three levels to calibrate the depth of a citizen science project.

Bounded

Data collection only. Students contribute, but have limited agency over the project.

Threshold

Some analysis or interpretation. Students begin to make meaning from the data.

Full Cycle

Co-design, dissemination, and evaluation. Students shape the work and its destination.
